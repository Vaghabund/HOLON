HOLON

A holistic exploration of interconnected systems and wholeness.

Understanding Wholeness

The concept of holon represents something that is simultaneously a whole and a part of a larger system. Each element exists independently while contributing to the greater structure.

Interconnected Systems

In nature, society, and technology, we find holons everywhere. Atoms form molecules, molecules form cells, cells form organisms. Each level maintains its identity while participating in something larger.

The Exhibition

This exhibition explores the boundaries between part and whole, individual and collective, autonomy and integration. Through visual and textual elements, we invite you to contemplate your own place in the nested hierarchies of existence.
